About Me 😊🌸

Hello, I’m Yue Wu! 🎉 I’m a passionate graduate student pursuing a Master of Fine Arts at Sichuan Normal University in Chengdu, China. 🌺 With a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Sciences from Southwest Petroleum University, I blend technical expertise with creative artistry to explore innovative solutions in Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), and Extended Reality (XR). 💻🎨 My work focuses on creating immersive experiences that address real-world challenges, like reducing stress through VR meditation 🌿 and enhancing classroom engagement with interactive VR avatars 👩‍🏫. I’m skilled in tools like Unity, Unreal Engine 5, and Blender, and I love filmmaking and digital design! 🎥✨ Explore my CV, projects, and portfolio to learn more about my journey and achievements! 🚀❤️
