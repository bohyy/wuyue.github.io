About Me 😊

With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Sciences from Southwest Petroleum University, I blend technical expertise with creative artistry to explore innovative solutions in Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), and Extended Reality (XR). 💻🎨

My work focuses on creating immersive experiences that address real-world challenges, like reducing stress through VR meditation 🌿 and enhancing classroom engagement with interactive VR avatars 👩‍🏫.

I'm skilled in tools like Unity, Unreal Engine 5, and Blender, and I love filmmaking and digital design! 🎥✨
